# BookLore Support                                                                                                                                                                                              
BookLore app is a mobile client for your self-hosted BookLore server.                                                                                                                                               
## Support                                                                                                                                                                            

For issues or feedback, please [open an issue](https://github.com/ila174/booklore-mobile/issues).

## Privacy Policy

BookLore mobile app connects to your self-hosted server. We do not operate any central servers, and we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or library contents.

Full privacy policy is available within the app under Settings > Legal.

## Terms of Use

Full terms of use are available within the app under Settings > Legal.
